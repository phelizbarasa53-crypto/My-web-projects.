Bible Verses To Ready When Life Seems Miserable

Psalms 23:1-2

The psalmist says; 1The Lord is my shepherd, I shall not want;
2He makes me lie down in green pastures

Explanations:

vs1: If we do focus on the Lord during our darkest moments of life, we shall not worry neither shall we lose hope.

Though the condition that we are in causes us to question God severally, we don't have to do so but wait for his timing. It is better for us to ask him for patience and endurance in what we are facing.

Just remember that for his glory to be shown, our Lord do allows some situations in our lives. Through that, we can see his power and strength in that situation.

The faith possessed by Meshach, Shadrack and Abednego was enough to sustain them and make them stiff never to worship the golden stature set by king Nebuchadnezzar

Not that they didn't know know the consequences but depended ultimately on the almighty. You can imagine that situation, you are seeing the furnace and even perceiving its hotness but you are still unshakable with your faith. That is how our Lord works. He did not prevent the trio from fire, but he was with them in the fire

Fellow believers, always know that God is there in all you face. He is your shepherd and will never leave his flock

vs2:The Lord makes me lie in green pastures.

In this scenario, though worried, though hopeless, our Lord comforts us in all we are in.

Breathren, it doesn't matter where life has pushed you, our Lord is a restorer just as he has said in Jeremiah 30:17: I will restore health to you, and your wounds I will heal.
